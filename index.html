We are Bicyclize

Providing traffic easing transport solutions with the help of active transport
Bicyclize was formed in order to help cities transform their public spaces to more human focused, attractive ones. Very often the catalyst for infrastructure appraisal grows from changing mobility patterns and increased demand for transport mode options. City cycling is not only ideal for urban mobility for the distances one can conveniently cover but also for its ability to integrate movement with place making factors.
