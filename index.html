Our Team

Peter Biczok, M.Sc.

Transport Planner
Peter drove public transport buses while saving for his economics degree. This turned out to be a valuable investment. After consulting technology for blue-chip financial companies in the City of London, he earned a masters degree in City and Transport Planning from the prestigious Bartlett School of Planning, University College London. He has worked on EU transport portfolios for the European Commission before founding Bicyclize in Amsterdam.

Andrew Reynolds

Urban Designer
Andrew Reynolds is as an Urban Designer and Planner. He is passionate about creating better public spaces for walking and cycling, and has a track record of delivering contextual and innovative design responses at a range of scales from local parks Australia and Rotterdam to working on complex multidisciplinary transport and infrastructure projects in the UK.
