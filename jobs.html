Engineering intern

We are rapidly expanding and continously looking for talent who are equally enthusiastic about improving cycling conditions across the world.

Bicyclize provides mobility measurement software to quantify the place of cycling in the city (pun intended). Interest in machine learning and neural networks

Team player

Selflearner, problem solver
